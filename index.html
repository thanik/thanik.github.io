about

I'm Thanik, a Thai game developer living in the UK. I just graduated MSC. Computer Games Technology from Abertay University. I was a web developer before but I left my job and pursuit my dream creating games that everyone in the world can play and have fun!

My interests are interactive experiences that can incorporate both visual and audio together. Also, I interested in UI/UX design as well.
